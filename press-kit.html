7Seven Studios is a one person studio founded in 2020 by Louis DiGiorno III. Although I was born in the 2000s, my childhood consisted of retro classics like Super Mario and Donkey Kong and I have strived to recreate games with that same nostalgic feeling. Town of Tinysville is my first major release that emulates the 80s and 90s. The original Town of Tinysville was made during a game jam in 2021 and was instantly a fan favorite. Four years later, in 2025, I decided to revive the project and create a bigger and better Town of Tinysville. This time around, I wanted to challenge myself. I made one of the hardest decisions at the beginning of development to keep the retro feel. I did this by reducing the game to a tiny color palette, with only 16 main colors.

Town of Tinysville Description

Town of Tinysville is a retro-themed deckbuilding city-builder where every placement matters. Build your dream town one card at a time while carefully managing your deck, grid-space, available resources, beauty, and town funds. Every decision ripples outward. One poorly placed building can collapse your carefully crafted village. Unlike traditional deckbuilders focused on combat, Tinysville challenges you to optimize growth, balance synergy, and adapt to an ever-changing town layout. Will you carefully plan a thriving metropolis, or will poor zoning and reckless expansion devastate your run?

Features

Building Interactions - Each building interacts with the structures around it. Positioning is key. Boost production with clever placement and synergies. Navigate powerful buffs and dangerous debuffs and discover intricate placement strategies that multiply your coin generation.

Upgradable Laws - Choose from 18 distinct laws, each with upgradable paths that permanently transform how your town functions mid-run. Strengthen existing strategies, impose new constraints, or radically alter your economy. As laws evolve, so does your gameplay strategy, forcing you to adapt your layout and rethink your priorities.

Unlimited Replayability - No two towns are the same. Each new run is randomly generated. Test your skills on 14 towns with distinct shapes and constraints and 7 difficulty levels each. Adaptation is survival.

Growing Collection - Cards discovered and played in your runs remain unlocked permanently. Experiment with new synergies, complete your collection, and refine your strategy across future attempts.

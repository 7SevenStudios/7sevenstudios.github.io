7Seven Studios is a one person studio founded in 2020 by Louis DiGiorno III. Although I was born in the 2000s, my childhood consisted of retro classics like Super Mario and Donkey Kong and I have strived to recreate games with that same nostalgic feeling. Town of Tinysville is my first major release that emulates the 80s and 90s. The original Town of Tinysville was made during a game jam in 2021 and was instantly a fan favorite. Four years later, in 2025, I decided to revive the project and create a bigger and better Town of Tinysville. This time around, I wanted to challenge myself. I made one of the hardest decisions at the beginning of development to keep the retro feel. I did this by reducing the game to a tiny color palette, only consisting of 15 different colors.


Town of Tinysville Description

What happens when you combine a card-driven city-builder with a classic roguelike? A uniquely relaxing yet engaging retro-themed experience! Place buildings while managing your deck grid-space, available resources, beauty, and of course, town funds. Upset the delicate balance and you'll devastate your run. Will you be the ultimate city planner?



Features:

Building Interactions - Each building interacts with eachother in unique ways. Discover intricate building placements to make generate even more coins! Watch out for building debuffs!

Unlimited Replayability- Whenever you start a new run, your town is randomly generated. Each run offers a new challenges to overcome!

Tons of New Challenges - With over 12 towns and 7 difficulty levels each, there will always be more challenges to overcome.

Collection - Cards you discover in runs will stay unlocked forever! Can you complete your entire collection?
